NSS - National Service Scheme
“Not me but YOU”


National Service Scheme is a student-organized program conducted by the Ministry of Youth and Human Resources and sponsored by the Government of India. Started in 1969, NSS has units across universities and schools in India, providing and At NIT-T, we ideate, organise, and execute socially responsible events nudging the younger community towards social welfare and well being.





NSS is mandatory for all freshers of NIT-T and included as a part of their academic curriculum. Volunteers have to complete designated hours in NSS to attain their passing grade. Every department has a session every week - organised by Executive Committee Members and Program Officers based on various topics and knowledge sharing sessions.


We also promote and execute government schemes like Jal Shakthi, Ek Bharath Shrestha Bharath, Swach Bharath, and conduct awareness sessions on personal hygiene and practices. As a part of the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan Scheme, NSS Volunteers regularly teach the children of REC middle school located inside the NIT-T campus. As a team, NSS also ensures that villages adopted by NIT-T get all needs, whichever can be done by the NSS NIT-T unit.

Vision

Nudge students to create a good community of like-minded people with social responsibility.

Mission

Make students understand how government schemes are designed and implemented for the welfare of urban people. Make sure students perceive the plans made by the Government properly, and spread awareness about it.
